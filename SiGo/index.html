STAY IN OR GO OUT?

A service app that provides entertainment options for staying in or going out. Sigo reduces the anxiety of how to spend time at home and lessen the ever scary FOMO (Fear of Missing Out). Additionally, the service provides a centralized hub for planning an evening for yourself or a group.
